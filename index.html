Customized Reminder

Customizing reminder, users will be benefited from:

  • Choose your most preferable reminder channel
  • Users can be reminded through bot-generated calls their due date
  • Choose your most preferable date and time to be reminded

Network Diagnostic Tools

Their feature will enable myIndiHome app to run Network Diagnostic and send the result to our users service to be analyzed and fixed. Fitur ini memberikan keuntungan untuk users berupa:

  • Kemudahan dalam pelaporan gangguan jaringan
  • Users tidak perlu menelpon atau mengunjungi customer service IndiHome untuk support
  • Users akan mendapat respon atas keluhan lebih cepat

E-Wallet Binding

This feature will let users to bind their e-wallets to myIndiHome account so that:

  • Users will no longer need to manually pay their bill
  • Users bills can be automatically pay with their e-wallet auto debet (?)
  • Decrease the chance of users forgetting to pay the bills
